Projects

My research topics include isolation of waterfowl pathogens and pathogenic mechanisms, neurodegenerative diseases, retinal diseases, bioinformatics, genomics, and transcriptomics.

Analyzing molecular changes in the retina of chick myopia model using spatial and single-cell transcriptomics

This project explores the molecular pathways involved in myopia progression using spatial and single-cell transcriptomic technologies.

Single-cell spatial transcriptomics in human brains of Alzheimer’s disease reveals neuronal subtypes that promote Aβ deposition

This study utilizes single-cell spatial transcriptomics to identify specific neuronal subtypes in Alzheimer’s disease that overexpress key genes promoting Aβ deposition, providing insights into the spatial and cellular mechanisms of the disease.

Establishment of RPA-LFD visual detection method for animal influenza A virus

A rapid and sensitive diagnostic tool for influenza A virus in animals using RPA-LFD technology.

Effects of resveratrol on antioxidant capacity and apoptosis of duck liver under acute heat stress conditions

Investigation of resveratrol’s protective effects against oxidative stress and apoptosis in waterfowl under heat stress.
