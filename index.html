Cuitian Peng

I got my master's degree from University of Chinese Academic of Sciences in July 2024. Previously, I got my B.S. degree from Zhongkai University of Agriculture and Engineering in 2021. My research spans the areas of Big data analysis, Spatial Transcriptomics, Single Cell Transcriptomics, Genomics, Neurodegeneration diseases, and Retina diseases.
