Cuitian Peng

I am currently a first-year PhD student at the UCL Institute of Ophthalmology. I earned my master's degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in July 2024 and my bachelor's degree from Zhongkai University of Agriculture and Engineering in 2021. My research focuses on Big Data Analysis, Spatial Transcriptomics, Single-Cell Transcriptomics, Genomics, Neurodegenerative Diseases, and Retinal Diseases. My PhD study is centered on dry AMD (age-related macular degeneration), aiming to identify aging-related key molecular pathways in dry AMD and explore strategies to reverse these pathways. Meanwhile, I have a strong interest in building and fine-tuning foundational models.
